## Overview

Get started with CompareTech in minutes. You create an account, set up your first documentation space, explore the interface, and publish your initial content. This guide walks you through each step with clear instructions.

<Callout kind="tip">
  New users receive a free tier with unlimited spaces and basic features. Upgrade for advanced collaboration and custom domains.
</Callout>

## Create Your Account

Follow these steps to sign up.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Visit the Signup Page" icon="user-plus">
    Go to [app.comparetech.com/signup](https://app.comparetech.com/signup).
  </Step>
  <Step title="Enter Your Details" icon="edit-3">
    Provide your email, create a strong password, and verify your account via email.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Complete Onboarding" icon="check-circle">
    Answer a few questions about your project to customize your dashboard.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Set Up Your First Documentation Space

Create a dedicated space for your project docs.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Create New Space" icon="plus">
    Click **New Space** from the dashboard and name it, like `MyProjectDocs`.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Configure Settings" icon="settings">
    Set visibility to public or private and add a description.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Add Your First Page" icon="file-plus">
    Create a page named `index.mdx` as your homepage.
  </Step>
</Steps>

Start authoring with MDX. Use components for rich docs.

<Tabs>

````mdx
```yaml

---
```
````
  </Tab>
  <Tab title="Headings and Text" icon="type">
    Structure content with proper hierarchy.

````mdx
```mdx

Start with H2, never H1.

### Subsections (H3)

Use bold **text** and *italics*.
```
````
